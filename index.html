Introduction

The ICT Joint Group Assignment explores sustainable garden projects to promote food security, community involvement, and digital literacy.

Goals

  • Investigate community garden initiatives.
  • Understand obstacles and success factors.

Approach

Research included government publications, NGO reports, academic papers, and case studies across South Africa.

Research Results

  • Soweto School Garden: Saves R2,500/month.
  • Durban Community Cooperative: Supports 35 households.

Visuals & Data

Home gardens contribute the largest share of local food supply in studied communities.

Suggestions

  • Encourage small household gardens.
  • Promote rainwater harvesting.

Conclusion

Community gardens show promise in addressing poverty and hunger.
